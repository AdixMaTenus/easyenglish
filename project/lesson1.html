Lesson 1: The Great Wall of China

Read the text and answer the questions below.

The Great Wall of China is considered one of the greatest wonders of the world. Its history goes back more than two thousand years, and the total length of the wall reaches over twenty-one thousand kilometers, crossing mountains, deserts, and grasslands. The wall was not built all at once, but in different periods, by several Chinese dynasties. The main purpose of the Great Wall was to protect the country from invasions by northern tribes. In addition to its military function, it also helped to control trade routes, including the famous Silk Road. Today, the Great Wall is a symbol of Chinese history and culture. It shows the strength and determination of the people who built it. Every year millions of tourists come to China to walk on the Great Wall, enjoy the landscapes around it, and learn more about the country’s rich past.
1) What was the main purpose of the Great Wall?
2) Which trade route was connected with the Wall?
3) What does the Great Wall symbolize today?
4) How many kilometers does the Wall stretch?
5) Why do tourists visit the Wall today?
6) The Great Wall was built by several dynasties.
7) The Wall was originally built for tourists.
